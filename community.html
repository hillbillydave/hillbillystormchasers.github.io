Hillbilly Storm Chasers Community Hub

A space for local support, storm‑safety resources, ministerial services, and community connection.

Welcome

Welcome to the Hillbilly Storm Chasers Community Hub — a place for people who need someone steady. This page is for neighbors, families, and anyone looking for honest support, practical help, and grounded guidance here in our corner of the world.

Community Services

Weddings & Ceremonies

As an ordained minister, Rev. David Blackwell offers simple, honest, and meaningful ceremonies:

  • Small, personal weddings
  • Outdoor and rural ceremonies
  • Custom vows and straightforward, no‑nonsense services

Funerals & Memorials

Respectful, grounded support for families during difficult times:

  • Graveside services and memorial readings
  • Simple, sincere words that honor the person’s life
  • Flexible, family‑centered approach — not tied to any one denomination

Blessings & Dedications

Ceremonies and moments of intention for everyday life:

  • Home blessings
  • Vehicle blessings (yes, including storm‑chaser rigs)
  • Storm‑season protection prayers and community dedications

Storm‑Chaser Outreach

Severe Weather Safety Tips

Local, experience‑based guidance for tornado season and rural severe weather:

  • Practical safety tips for families and drivers
  • Basic preparedness for power loss and road closures
  • Common‑sense advice from real‑world chasing, not theory

Community Alerts

During major weather events, this hub can serve as a central place to share:

  • Local impact notes and conditions
  • Road, power, and access concerns
  • Recovery and assistance information when possible

Volunteer Opportunities

When storms hit, neighbors help neighbors. This space can highlight:

  • Informal cleanup and support efforts
  • Ways to check on vulnerable folks nearby
  • Simple, real things people can do to help
