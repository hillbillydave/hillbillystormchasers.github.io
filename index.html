Welcome to Hillbilly Storm Chasers

Hillbilly Storm Chasers is a combined storm‑chasing, media, and frontier‑research initiative based in Wyandotte, Oklahoma. We document severe weather across the Midwest, develop independent theoretical physics frameworks, and explore the deep connections between storms, nonlinear dynamics, and the informational structure of the universe.

Storm Chasing

From supercells to tornado intercepts, we chase and document severe weather across Oklahoma, Kansas, Missouri, and the surrounding region. Our gallery features real chase photos, field captures, and behind‑the‑scenes moments from the road.
