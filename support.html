Support the Chase

Hillbilly Storm Chasers is a fully independent storm‑chasing and research operation based in Wyandotte, Oklahoma. Every mile we drive, every storm we document, and every piece of research we publish is powered directly by community support.

Your contribution helps keep us on the road — gathering real‑world data, capturing severe weather, and advancing frontier research in emergent time, informational geometry, and nonlinear dynamics.

Your support directly funds:

  • Fuel for storm‑chasing deployments
  • Camera gear — lenses, mounts, batteries, stabilization
  • Data plans — radar, GPS tracking, real‑time analysis
  • Safety equipment — radios, first‑aid, emergency supplies
  • Research tools — ODIM‑U, QSTF, informational‑geometry modeling
  • Website & media hosting — chase logs, research, updates
