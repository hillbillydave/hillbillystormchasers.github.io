Support the Chase

Hillbilly Storm Chasers is a fully independent storm‑chasing and research operation based in Wyandotte, Oklahoma. Every mile we drive, every storm we document, and every piece of research we publish is powered directly by community support. Your contribution helps keep us on the road and in the field — gathering real‑world data, capturing severe weather, and advancing frontier research in emergent time, informational geometry, and nonlinear dynamics.

Your support directly funds:

  • Fuel for storm‑chasing deployments across Oklahoma, Kansas, Missouri, and beyond
  • Camera gear including lenses, mounts, batteries, and stabilization equipment
  • Data plans for radar access, GPS tracking, and real‑time storm analysis
  • Safety equipment such as radios, first‑aid kits, and emergency supplies
  • Research tools for ODIM‑U, Quiet‑Scalar Time, and informational‑geometry modeling
  • Website and media hosting for sharing chase logs, research, and community updates
